# 🌿 Verdant
### Engineered for Speed. Built to Win.

*A premium football boot landing experience designed to make people stop scrolling and start staring.*

🔴 **[See it live](https://verdant-peach-zeta.vercel.app)**

---

## 💭 The Idea

Most product pages just sit there. Verdant doesn't.

As you scroll, the boot itself comes alive — rotating into view frame by frame, perfectly synced to how fast or slow you scroll. It's the same kind of scroll-reactive product reveal you'd expect from a flagship brand launch page, except built entirely from scratch. Not a video. Not a GIF. A custom animation engine reacting to you in real time. That single detail is what turns a page people skim into a page people remember.

## ✨ What's Inside

🎞️ **A Living Hero Section**
The boot rotates into frame as you scroll, with a cinematic loading sequence that builds anticipation instead of showing a blank screen.

🎨 **Three Colorways, Instantly**
Switch colorways and watch the entire scene shift color in real time — no reload, no extra assets, no delay.

🕹️ **A Real Product Configurator**
Visitors explore performance specs — Speed, Control, Traction — pick a size, and choose a colorway, all through smooth, app-quality interactions.

📝 **A Built-In Reservation Flow**
A working pre-order form with its own success state, ready to be connected to a real backend whenever you are.

📱 **Flawless on Every Screen**
A floating navigation bar that tracks where you are on the page, with a clean slide-out menu on mobile.

## 🏆 Why It Works

Anyone can put a photo of a shoe on a webpage. Almost nobody builds a page that performs. Verdant is built the way a flagship brand would build it — fast, intentional, and engineered down to the easing curve of every scroll interaction. It's the kind of site that makes a visitor pause before they've even read the headline.
